Avoid Youtube cookies

Drag a link below to your bookmarks, and click on it when you land on a Youtube video page.

From youtube.com URL: NoCookieTube

New From consent.youtube.com URL: NoConsentTube

Trying to keep your privacy footprint as low as possible on Youtube is hard. There is a consent middle page, and not being logged in is actively punished, by multiple popups.

This small player is a simple iframe embed, so you can watch Youtube without popups or consenting. It also requests videos from the youtube-nocookie domain, so no cookies are stored.
